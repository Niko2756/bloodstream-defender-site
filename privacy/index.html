Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Overview

Bloodstream Defender does not directly collect personal information, does not track players, does not show third-party ads, and does not use third-party analytics.

Offline Play and Purchases

Bloodstream Defender can be played offline after download. The app has no in-app purchases; once purchased, the game and future updates are included.

Game Center

Bloodstream Defender uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards and challenges. Game Center data is handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policies and account settings.

Support Emails

If you contact support by email, your email address and message contents will be used only to respond to your request.

Children's Privacy

Bloodstream Defender is designed as an arcade game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated if the app changes how it handles data. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to: bloodstreamdefendersupport@gmail.com
